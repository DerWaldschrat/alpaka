Detailed Description

template<>
struct alpaka::wait::traits::CurrentThreadWaitFor< event::EventCpuAsync >

The CPU device event thread wait trait specialization.

Waits until the event itself and therefore all tasks preceding it in the stream it is enqueued to have been completed. If the event is not enqueued to a stream the method returns immediately.
